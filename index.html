🔥 Free Fire IGL Guide 🔥

🎯 Squad Roles

🔫 Assaulter:
Front line player, fights first.
Good aim + aggressive gameplay.

⚡ Primary Rusher:
Push enemy squad fast.
Use SMG + shotgun.

🔥 Secondary Rusher:
Backup rusher.
Support and revive teammates.

🎯 Sniper:
Long range fights.
Give cover and knock enemies.

🧬 Best Character Skills

Assaulter: Alok, Chrono

Rusher: Jota, Kelly

Sniper: Laura, Rafael

Use combination of speed + healing + accuracy.

📍 Positioning Guide

IGL: Always center position

Rusher: Front push

Sniper: High ground / back

Support: Middle cover

Always stay in formation and don't rush solo ❌
